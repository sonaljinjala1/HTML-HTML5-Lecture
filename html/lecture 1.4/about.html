ABOUT US

HelpGuide.org is an independent nonprofit that runs one of the world’s leading mental health websites. Each month, millions of people from all around the world turn to us for trustworthy information they can use to improve their mental health and make healthy changes.


  • Meet our Team

    The HelpGuide team is a passionate group of people dedicated to providing you with empowering, evidence-based mental health information, resources, and tools.

  • Transparency you can trust

    HelpGuide is a non-profit, tax-exempt 501(c)3 organization incorporated under the laws of the State of California. As a small nonprofit that runs one of the world’s top mental health websites, we are proud of our efficiency and outsized impact. HelpGuide is recognized by Guidestar as a financially accountable and transparent organization
